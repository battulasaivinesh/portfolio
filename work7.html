Aarohan 2019

The Annual Techno Management Fest of NIT Durgapur.


About The Project

A project developed as a Head, Web Design and Creative Team under Centre for Cognitive Activities, NIT Durgapur. The project was to develop a website depicting the theme of the fest. At this project, I was the Head of Web Design and Creative Team. The theme of the fest was Progressive Energetics. We wanted the website to be minimalistic and simple. The main challenge was to make this website responsive.
